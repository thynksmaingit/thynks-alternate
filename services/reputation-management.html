Questions, Answered

Before you book the session

+Can you remove negative reviews?

Only when they violate platform guidelines. Legitimate negative reviews can't simply be erased, but the right response can protect trust.

+Is review automation allowed?

Asking customers for honest reviews is generally allowed. Incentives, fake reviews, and deceptive gating are not. We design ethical systems.

+How quickly should reviews be answered?

Fast enough that prospects see an active, attentive business. For negative reviews, speed and tone matter.

+What platforms matter most?

It depends on the industry. Google is usually critical, but healthcare, legal, real estate, restaurants, and home services each have their own trust ecosystem.
