Questions, Answered

Before you book the session

+Is email still worth it?

Yes, especially when paid media costs rise and organic reach gets unreliable. Owned audiences create leverage.

+Do we need a huge list?

No. A smaller list with strong intent can outperform a large list that barely knows you.

+Can you write the emails?

Yes. Strategy, copy, flow logic, subject lines, testing, and templates are all part of the work.

+What's the difference between email marketing and marketing automation?

Email is the message. Automation is the decision system that knows who gets what, when, and why.
