Platform Philosophy

The right two or three beat being everywhere.

  • LinkedIn — authority, B2B trust, founder voice, complex-sale education
  • Instagram — brand memory, visual proof, lifestyle, product, local trust
  • TikTok — reach, personality, cultural fluency, fast creative testing
  • Facebook — local visibility, community, reviews, retargeting, older demos
  • YouTube Shorts — search-adjacent discovery and evergreen video snippets
Questions, Answered

Before you book the session

+Which platforms should we be on?

The right two or three matter more than pretending to be everywhere. We choose based on your audience, offer, team capacity, and creative strengths.

+Do you create video?

Yes. We can script, direct, edit, repurpose, and build repeatable formats.

+Can you work with our in-house person?

Yes. We can be the strategy and production layer while your team handles day-to-day community, or we can operate the full channel.

+How often should we post?

Often enough to build memory, not so often that quality collapses. Cadence is platform-specific.

+Do you handle paid social?

Yes. Organic and paid work best together: organic reveals what resonates; paid scales what deserves more light.
