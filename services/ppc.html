Questions, Answered

Before you book the session

+How much budget do we need?

Enough to generate meaningful conversion data. The exact number depends on industry, location, offer, margin, and sales cycle.

+Do you build landing pages too?

Yes. Sending paid traffic to a weak page is one of the fastest ways to dim a good campaign.

+Can you manage existing ad accounts?

Yes. In many cases, keeping historical data is useful. We audit first, then decide whether to restructure, rebuild, or preserve what's working.

+What platforms do you recommend?

The audience decides. Search captures intent. Social creates and retargets demand. LinkedIn works for the right B2B economics. YouTube can scale education and trust.

+How do you measure success?

Qualified opportunities, CAC, ROAS where relevant, conversion quality, landing-page performance, and pipeline movement — not clicks alone.
