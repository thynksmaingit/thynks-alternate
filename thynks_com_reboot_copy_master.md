## Design system translation
- Use editorial asymmetry and large-scale headline typography.
- Use yellow as the intelligent spark, not as decoration.
- Avoid heavy borders; separate sections through tonal background shifts.
- Use intelligence cards with short, high-signal copy.
- Keep body copy lean and confident.
- Give each page one strong visual metaphor tied to light, signal, circuits, clarity, or momentum.
