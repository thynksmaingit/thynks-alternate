Questions, Answered

Before you book the session

+What's a good ROAS?

It depends on margin, repeat purchase, fulfillment cost, and lifecycle value. We model the economics before chasing a number.

+Should we move off Shopify?

Usually no. Shopify is enough for most brands. Custom builds make sense only when requirements truly demand it.

+Can email become a larger revenue channel?

Yes, if lifecycle flows, segmentation, offers, and deliverability are built properly.

+Do you handle creative testing?

Yes. Creative is often the biggest lever in paid social, and it needs a testing system.
